Compact Music

As a family owned company, Compact Music has been a leader in the Ottawa used-music industry since the 1980’s. Their heavy vinyl collection helped them outlast media giants for decades. Currently they have two locations located on Bank Street, and plan on expanding.

The logo represents the best qualities of Compact Music’s identity: the modern, yet old school, values of the company and their root: vinyl. The logo’s blend between old school imagery and a modern sans serif font creates a harmony while being able to hold up with today’s industry standards.

There were no restrictions given by the client except that it needed to stand out in mall signage. I did 2 rounds of logo concept sketches and once a concept I liked was chosen I did 3 rounds of refinement before going digital. I used classic rock imagery for inspiration and came up with a CM monogram play on a vinyl sleeve. I included an elite gold in the colour pallet in order to represent rarities and collectibles that a used record store may sell, such as gold records. I paired this colour with a complementary purple in order to really make the brand stand out.
