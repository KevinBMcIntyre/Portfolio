Bond Paralax • Motion

Movies have always inspired me in both my everyday life and my career. One franchise in particular is 007, James Bond. I love the action, the stories, the thrills and especially the opening title sequences. My favourite being Skyfall’s underwater adventure. This title sequence inspired me to recreate it in my own style while mixing in some classic bond swagger. For the song I went with what was originally going to be the opening song for Spectre by Radiohead but got cut at the last minute.
